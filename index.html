About Me

Hi I am Mahabali , a.k.a Bali,Indrasenan or Maveli. A passionate devotee of Lord Vishnu and also a Daitya king descended from sage Kashyapa. Nowadays, I come from patala once every year to visit the people of my kingdom. Literally I am an immortal!, because I possesed amrita which brought me back to life after my death in one of the wars between suras and asuras. And finally after so many wars, I won heaven and Earth. But the suras a.k.a Devas, approached Vishnu and he came up with a tactial solution. He disguised himself as Vamana and asked me for 3 steps of land. At last I offered my own head as the third step since he had already taken all of heaven in one step and all of earth in another. And hence I was sent to patala a.k.a netherworld.
